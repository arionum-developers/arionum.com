Download THE wallet

Get your wallet now!

Your ARO is secure as long as your keys are safe. Download one of the light wallet applications to store your keys safe and sound in your own possession. Don't forget to encrypt your wallet file and no one will be able to steal from you without knowing the correct password, even if they have access to your wallet.aro file.

Light wallet GUI

The Windows GUI light wallet client is the easiest way to get started with ARIONUM. The Windows GUI light wallet is the preferred client for most Windows users. This is the best client to start with, but it's not suitable for masternode operators.

Source code: https://github.com/arionum/lightWalletGUI

Binary file: https://www.arionum.com/LightArionumD/setup.exe

Crossplatform wallet client

The Cross-platform wallet client is currently under revision by members of our core developers team and will be released soon :)

Light wallet CLI

A Powerful command-line client fully coded in PHP allows you to access extended features and commands. If you are an advanced user or masternode operator, this is the wallet client for you.

Source code: https://github.com/arionum/lightWalletCLI
