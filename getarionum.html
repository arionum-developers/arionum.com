Mine ARIONUM!

Help process new transactions on the blockchain and release new ARO! Cryptocurrency mining needs a computer and a special program /miner/ which is trying to solve complicated mathematical problems - blocks.

The current reward is 1000 coins per block and each month the block reward is reduced by 10 ARO. Arionum, with it's unique hybrid mining system, is CPU and GPU mineable. 33% of the block reward goes to masternodes and the rest belongs to the miners.
