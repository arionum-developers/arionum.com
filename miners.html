1. Choose your miner application

To mine ARO you need to understand that ARIONUM has 2 different mining rounds : a CPU round and a GPU round.

The names for these rounds may be misleading as both rounds can be mined with either GPU or CPU. CPUs and GPUs can mine on both of the rounds and hashrates will be very different on each round. In general you will have a much better hash rate in the GPU round than the CPU round due to different parameters of hashing algorithm and to the difficulty.

To maximise your mining rewards, it is important to have good hash rates in both the GPU and CPU rounds. All available mining software will allow you to mine on both the CPU round and the GPU round, but not all of them will allow you to mine with both your GPU and CPU.
